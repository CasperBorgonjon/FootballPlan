================================================================
SOCCER OFF-SEASON PLAN — 12 WEEK BLOCK (FULLBACK)
================================================================

OVERVIEW
- 3 phases, 10 tracked weeks (Phase 1: 1–3, Phase 2: 4–6, Phase 3: 7–10)
- 6 training days/week, SUN = full rest
- Deload weeks: Week 4 and Week 8 (drop volume 40%, keep intensity)


================================================================
TRAINING PLAN
================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 1 — Base & Movement Quality (Weeks 1–3)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Coach note: Build aerobic base and movement foundations. Weights
moderate — perfect form now. Every rep controlled and deliberate.

MON — Strength — Lower Body (focus: Strength)
  01 Back Squat                3×8     65–70% 1RM, feel movement
  02 Romanian Deadlift         3×10    slow 3s eccentric, hinge focus
  03 Bulgarian Split Squat     3×8     each leg, bodyweight only
  04 Nordic Hamstring Curl     3×4     slow lower, hands to return
  05 Single-Leg Calf Raise     3×12    each leg, full range

TUE — Aerobic Base — Steady Run (focus: Endurance)
  01 Easy run                  1×30min 65–70% max HR, comfortable
  02 Strides                   4×60m   after run, relaxed accel
  03 Walking hip circles       2×10    each direction
  04 Quad + hip flexor stretch 3×45s   each side
  05 Calf stretch (wall)       3×45s   each leg

WED — Strength — Upper & Core (focus: Strength)
  01 Barbell Bench Press       3×10    65–70% 1RM, full ROM
  02 Pull-Ups                  3×5     full ROM — band if needed
  03 Barbell Row               3×10    bent-over, controlled
  04 DB Overhead Press         3×12    10kg DBs, no arching
  05 Dead Bug                  3×8     each side, slow controlled
  06 Copenhagen Plank          3×15s   each side, groin builder

THU — Speed — Mechanics Only (focus: Speed)
  01 A-skip                    3×20m   slow, deliberate, form first
  02 B-skip                    3×20m   extend and claw ground
  03 Falling starts            5×15m   lean until you go, relaxed
  04 Acceleration sprints      6×20m   85% effort — not flat out
  05 Lateral shuffle           3×10m   each direction, low hips
  06 Box jumps                 3×4     moderate height, focus landing

FRI — Conditioning — Aerobic Base (focus: Endurance)
  01 Tempo run                 3×5min  75–80% HR, 2min jog rest
  02 Bounding                  3×20m   build power, full extension
  03 Lateral band walks        3×12    each dir, hip activation
  04 Plank hold                3×40s   quality over duration
  05 Hip flexor stretch        3×45s   couch stretch each side

SAT — Full-Body Power & Mobility (focus: Power)
  01 Squat Jump                3×5     bodyweight, land softly
  02 DB Romanian Deadlift      3×10    10kg DBs, hinge pattern
  03 Pull-Up Negatives         3×4     5s lowering, tendon adaptation
  04 Broad Jump                3×4     focus takeoff mechanics
  05 Thoracic rotation         2×10    each side, open upper back
  06 Full body stretch         1×15min hip, hamstring, calf, shoulder


----------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 2 — Strength & Speed Development (Weeks 4–6)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Coach note: Load goes up, rest periods more structured. Sprint
sessions hard — full recovery between reps. Where you get faster.

MON — Strength — Lower Power (focus: Strength)
  01 Back Squat                4×6     78–80% 1RM, controlled
  02 Romanian Deadlift         4×8     pause 1s at bottom
  03 Bulgarian Split Squat     3×8     each leg, add DBs
  04 Nordic Hamstring Curl     3×5     slow lower, small pause top
  05 Single-Leg Calf Raise     3×12    add DB if easy

TUE — Sprint — Max Speed Development (focus: Speed)
  01 Dynamic warmup            1×10min skips, high knees, leg swings
  02 Flying 20s                6×20m   build 20m, sprint 20m, 3min rest
  03 Top-speed runs            5×40m   100% effort, walk back rest
  04 Sprint + decelerate       5×30m   hard stop, knee control
  05 Lateral sprint + plant    5×10m   each direction

WED — HIIT — Aerobic Power (focus: Endurance)
  01 30-30 intervals           10×30/30s 90% max HR on effort
  02 Hill sprints (or stairs)  6×10s   all-out, walk down rest
  03 Squat jumps               3×6     between interval sets
  04 Foam roll                 1×10min quads, calves, ITB, glutes
  05 Stretch                   1×10min hamstring + hip flexor focus

THU — Strength — Upper & Core (focus: Strength)
  01 Barbell Bench Press       4×6     78–80% 1RM, explosive push
  02 Weighted Pull-Ups         3×5     add weight via bag/belt
  03 Barbell Row               4×8     squeeze at top 1s
  04 DB Lateral Raise          3×12    10kg, controlled
  05 Pallof Press              3×10    each side, rotational
  06 Copenhagen Plank          3×22s   each side

FRI — Conditioning — Match Simulation (focus: Endurance)
  01 10-10-10 run              8× 10s sprint/10s jog/10s walk
  02 Tempo run                 3×6min  80% HR, 2min rest
  03 Reactive lateral shuffle  4×8m    change direction on cue
  04 Single-leg RDL            3×8     each leg, DB, balance focus
  05 Hip 90-90 stretch         2×60s   each side

SAT — Explosive Power & Mobility (focus: Power)
  01 Squat (explosive)         4×4     80% 1RM, drive hard out of hole
  02 Depth Jump                3×4     step off box, react on landing
  03 Broad Jump                4×5     maximum effort
  04 Pull-Up + L-Sit hold      3× 5+10s upper body + core combo
  05 Full mobility session     1×20min hip flexors, ankles, thoracic


----------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 3 — Peak Speed & Pre-Season Readiness (Weeks 7–10)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Coach note: Sharpen everything. High speed, low volume. Feel fast
and light. Don't add load — express what you've built.

MON — Strength — Peak Lower (focus: Strength)
  01 Back Squat                4×3     85–88% 1RM, explosive intent
  02 Romanian Deadlift         3×5     controlled, heavier
  03 Bulgarian Split Squat     3×6     each leg, heavy DBs
  04 Nordic Hamstring Curl     3×6     maintain every week
  05 Single-Leg Calf Raise     3×10    weighted

TUE — Sprint — Maximum Velocity (focus: Speed)
  01 Sprint mechanics warmup   1×10min A/B skips, wickets
  02 Flying 30s                5×30m   30m build, 30m max, 4min rest
  03 5m explosive starts       8×5m    react start, pure acceleration
  04 Agility: T-drill          5×1     record time, full rest
  05 Deceleration runs         4×30m   sprint + hard stop, knee control

WED — Conditioning — High Intensity (focus: Endurance)
  01 5-minute run test         1×5min  max distance — benchmark
  02 40-20 intervals           8× 40s on/20s off, 95% max HR
  03 Bounding                  3×20m   explosive, full extension
  04 Lateral hurdle jumps      3×8     each direction, reactive
  05 Cooldown jog + stretch    1×15min

THU — Strength + Recovery (focus: Recovery)
  01 Barbell Bench Press       3×4     83–85% 1RM, peak strength
  02 Pull-Ups                  3×6     weighted, smooth
  03 Easy jog                  1×15min very easy — flush the legs
  04 Foam rolling              1×12min quads, calves, ITB, glutes
  05 Contrast shower           1×8min  2min hot/1min cold × 3

FRI — Activation — Pre-Season Prep (focus: Speed)
  01 Short sprint activation   5×15m   80% effort, sharp not hard
  02 Lateral cuts              4×5m    each direction, sharp plant
  03 Squat jumps               3×5     light and reactive
  04 Pull-Ups                  3×5     smooth, no strain
  05 Full stretch + breathwork 1×15min calm the nervous system

SAT — Time Trial & Full Fitness Test (focus: Endurance)
  01 Dynamic warmup            1×15min full body activation
  02 30m sprint (3 attempts)   3×30m   record best vs Week 1
  03 Vertical jump test        3×1     record best
  04 12-minute Cooper run      1×12min max distance — VO2 proxy
  05 Cooldown + stretch        1×15min you earned it

NUTRITION
================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------
TRAINING DAY — Macros
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Calories: 3,050 kcal
  Protein:  175 g
  Carbs:    370 g
  Fat:      85 g

PRE-SESSION (60–90 min before) — fast energy, no crash
  Carb sources:    Oats/oatmeal, white rice/pasta, banana/dates,
                   rice cakes, white bread + jam, fruit juice (small)
  Protein sources: Whole eggs/egg whites, low-fat Greek yogurt,
                   whey shake, turkey slices
  Add-ons:         Honey, black coffee/green tea, creatine (5g),
                   pinch of salt
  Avoid:           High-fat foods, high-fiber meals, dairy-heavy,
                   spicy food, carbonated drinks

POST-SESSION (within 45 min) — rapid recovery
  Protein:        Whey (fast), chicken breast, tuna/white fish,
                  egg whites, low-fat milk
  Carbs:          White rice, sweet potato, banana/mango/pineapple,
                  rice cakes, white pasta
  Electrolytes:   Sodium (salt pinch), potassium (banana, coconut
                  water), magnesium (pumpkin seeds), light sports drink
  Avoid:          High-fat meats, alcohol, skipping this meal

MAIN MEALS (lunch & dinner) — balanced performance fuel
  Protein:        Chicken breast/thigh, salmon/mackerel, lean beef/
                  turkey, eggs, lentils/chickpeas
  Complex carbs:  Brown/white rice, sweet potato, quinoa, pasta,
                  whole grain bread, oats
  Vegetables:     Broccoli, spinach/kale, peppers, zucchini,
                  tomatoes, carrots, beetroot
  Healthy fats:   Avocado, olive oil, almonds/walnuts, nut butters,
                  fatty fish

SNACKS (between meals) — bridge gaps, maintain energy
  High-protein:   Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, hard-boiled eggs,
                  protein bar, turkey rolls
  Carb-based:     Banana/apple, rice cakes + honey, low-sugar
                  granola, dates/raisins (small)
  Fat + protein:  Almonds + jerky, peanut butter on rice cakes,
                  hummus + veggies, trail mix

NIGHT RECOVERY (30–60 min before bed) — overnight muscle repair
  Slow proteins:  Cottage cheese, casein, full-fat Greek yogurt,
                  warm milk, quark/skyr
  Optional fats:  Almond butter (1 tbsp), walnuts, chia seeds
  Sleep support:  Chamomile tea, magnesium glycinate, tart cherry
                  juice, small amount of honey
  Avoid:          Large carb-heavy meals, caffeine after 2pm,
                  alcohol, heavy fats late at night


----------------------------------------------------------------
REST DAY — Macros
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Calories: 2,400 kcal
  Protein:  160 g
  Carbs:    240 g
  Fat:      80 g

MORNING (on waking) — refuel gently
  Protein:        3 whole eggs scrambled, Greek yogurt, cottage
                  cheese, smoked salmon
  Carbs:          2 slices sourdough, oats (smaller portion),
                  fruit (1 piece)
  Healthy fats:   ½ avocado, olive oil drizzle, handful of nuts

LUNCH (midday) — lean & balanced
  Protein:        Tuna/chicken/eggs, legumes (lentil soup,
                  chickpeas), white fish
  Carbs (reduced):½ cup brown rice, 1 sweet potato, 1 slice whole
                  grain bread
  Vegetables:     Large mixed salad, steamed broccoli/greens,
                  roasted peppers

DINNER (evening) — lean protein, light carbs
  Protein:        White fish/turkey, lean beef (small), eggs/tofu
  Vegetables:     Stir-fried mixed veg, big green salad, roasted
                  root vegetables
  Light carbs:    ½ cup lentils, small quinoa, 1 small sweet potato

SNACKS (as needed) — keep protein up
  Best:           Cottage cheese + berries, hard-boiled eggs,
                  water-based protein shake, apple + almond butter,
                  plain Greek yogurt
  Avoid:          High-carb snacks (crisps, bread), sugary drinks/
                  juices, large portions, takeaways/processed food


----------------------------------------------------------------
CORE NUTRITION PRINCIPLES
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Carb cycle:    High carbs on hard days. Cut ~40% on rest days.
                 Protein stays constant.
  Meal timing:   Eat every 3–4 hrs. Never skip post-session.
  Hydration:     3–4L on training days. Soccer sweat loss is high.
  Sleep = gains: 8–9 hours. Most adaptation happens during sleep.


================================================================
RECOVERY
================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------
SLEEP — most underused performance tool in football
----------------------------------------------------------------
NIGHTLY TARGETS (CRITICAL)
  Duration:     8–9 hours every night. Non-negotiable.
  Consistency:  Same bedtime/wake time — even weekends.
  Environment:  Cool room (65–68°F), fully dark, no screens 30 min
                before bed.
  Pre-sleep:    No caffeine after 2pm. Magnesium glycinate 300mg
                30 min before bed.

SLEEP & SOCCER PERFORMANCE (IMPORTANT)
  Reaction time:  Sleep-deprived players 20–30% slower decisions.
  Sprint speed:   Top speed drops after one night under 7 hrs.
  Injury risk:    Under 8 hrs = 1.7× more likely to be injured.
  Track it:       Sleep Cycle, Apple/Google Health. Log hours.

NAP PROTOCOL (OPTIONAL)
  When:      After lunch on double-session/very hard days.
  Duration:  20 min (power) or 90 min (full cycle). Nothing between.
  Timing:    Never after 3pm — wrecks night sleep.

----------------------------------------------------------------
BODY CARE & RECOVERY
----------------------------------------------------------------
COLD & CONTRAST THERAPY (HIGH IMPACT)
  Cold shower:    2–3 min cold at end of shower — daily post-training.
  Ice bath:       10–12 min at 54–59°F — within 1hr post-session.
  Contrast:       2 min hot / 1 min cold × 3 rounds.
  Avoid:          Ice baths after strength — blunt muscle adaptation.

FOAM ROLLING & SOFT TISSUE (DAILY)
  Morning:        10 min — quads, calves, hams, ITB, glutes, T-spine.
  Post-session:   5 min on whatever you trained.
  Tools:          Medium-density foam roller + lacrosse ball.
  Massage:        Once/month minimum.

DELOAD WEEKS (EVERY 4TH WEEK)
  When:    Week 4 and Week 8.
  How:     Drop volume 40%. Keep weight/intensity. Fewer sets.
  Why:     Supercompensation happens during deloads.
  Active:  Light jog, swim, cycle, mobility. Not full rest.

----------------------------------------------------------------
PREHAB & INJURY PREVENTION
----------------------------------------------------------------
HAMSTRINGS (CRITICAL — #1 SOCCER INJURY)
  Why:               Most common time-loss injury in pro soccer.
  Nordic curls:      3×6 twice/week — best hamstring prevention.
  Eccentric:         Always control lowering phase.
  Flexibility:       90-90 + standing stretch, 45s holds, daily.
  Load management:   Never max sprint without thorough warmup.

GROIN & ADDUCTORS (HIGH RISK)
  Why:               Lateral moves + crossing load groin.
  Copenhagen plank:  3×20s each side — gold standard.
  Adductor squeeze:  3×15 ball between knees — daily.
  Hip flexor:        Couch stretch 2×45s each side — daily.

ANKLES & KNEES (HIGH)
  Ankle circles:     30 each direction — before every session.
  Single-leg balance:30s each leg on foam pad — proprioception.
  Terminal knee ext: 3×15 with band — protects patellar tendon.
  Calf raises:       Single-leg slow eccentric 3×15 — Achilles.

SHOULDERS & NECK (MEDIUM)
  Why:               Headers + duels stress neck/shoulders.
  Neck isometrics:   4 directions × 10s holds — 3×/week.
  Band pull-aparts:  3×20 — daily, no equipment.
  Rotator cuff:      Y-T-W + ext rotation — 3×12 twice/week.

----------------------------------------------------------------
MENTAL PERFORMANCE
----------------------------------------------------------------
VISUALIZATION (HIGH IMPACT)
  What:       Mental rehearsal of movements/decisions/scenarios.
  When:       5–10 min before bed or morning. Daily.
  How:        Close eyes. First-person pitch view. Replay best
              moments. Feel best pass/sprint/finish.
  Game prep:  Night before match — visualize assignments, runs,
              winning duels.

STRESS & CORTISOL (RECOVERY KILLER)
  Why:        Chronic stress = high cortisol = slow recovery,
              muscle breakdown, bad sleep.
  Breathwork: 4-7-8 — inhale 4s/hold 7s/exhale 8s, 4 rounds pre-bed.
  Social:     Time with friends/family lowers cortisol.
  Nature:     20 min outside (no phone) reduces cortisol.
  Avoid:      Heavy social media/news spikes cortisol.

FOCUS & PRE-SESSION ROUTINE
  Ritual:     Same warmup music/movements/cues. Trains nervous
              system to switch on.
  Process:    Focus on what you control: pressing, movement, touch.
  Journal:    3 min post-training — 1 thing done well, 1 to improve.

----------------------------------------------------------------
TRACKING & TESTING
----------------------------------------------------------------
STRENGTH TESTING (START OF EACH PHASE)
  When:        1RM at weeks 1, 5, 9 — before each new phase.
  Key lifts:   Back squat, deadlift, bench press.
  Why:         %-based training only accurate with current 1RM.
  Log:         Date, weight, sets, reps, RPE (1–10). Strong/JEFIT.

ATHLETIC TESTING (EVERY 4 WEEKS)
  30m sprint:    Primary speed metric. 3 attempts, record best.
  Vertical jump: Standing vertical — lower body power.
  505 agility:   5m in, turn, 5m out — change-of-direction speed.
  Yo-Yo test:    Intermittent fitness — match aerobic capacity.

BODY & RECOVERY MONITORING (WEEKLY)
  Bodyweight:    Daily, same time. Track 7-day average.
  Resting HR:    Elevated AM = under-recovered. Pull back.
  HRV:           Most accurate readiness metric. Apple Health/Garmin.
  Subjective:    Daily 1–10: energy, soreness, mood.
  Progress pics: Front/side/back, same lighting, every 4 weeks.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SUPPLEMENTATION
----------------------------------------------------------------
TIER 1 — TAKE THESE (EVIDENCE-BASED)
  Creatine monohydrate:  5g daily, every day. Sprint power + recovery.
  Vitamin D3:            2000–4000 IU daily. Immunity, T, recovery.
  Magnesium glycinate:   300–400mg before bed. Sleep + cramps.
  Omega-3 (fish oil):    2–3g EPA/DHA daily. Inflammation, joints.
  Protein powder:        Whey post, casein at night — only if needed.

TIER 2 — CONSIDER (MODERATE EVIDENCE)
  Caffeine:         3–5mg/kg, 30–45 min pre-session.
  Beta-alanine:     3.2g/day. Reduces fatigue in HIIT.
  Beetroot juice:   300–500ml, 2–3 hrs pre. VO2 efficiency.
  Tart cherry:      8oz before bed. Reduces soreness/inflammation.
  Electrolyte mix:  Hard days — sodium/potassium/magnesium.

SKIP THESE (SAVE YOUR MONEY)
  Most pre-workouts:    Caffeine + filler. Just drink coffee.
  BCAAs:                Redundant if protein adequate.
  Fat burners:          Off-season is for building.
  Test boosters:        No credible evidence.
  Proprietary blends:   Underdosed, hidden amounts.

TIMING GUIDE
  Morning:       Vitamin D3 + Omega-3 + Zinc (with breakfast)
  Pre-session:   Caffeine (45 min before), Beta-alanine, Creatine,
                 Beetroot juice
  Post-session:  Whey protein, Creatine (if not pre)
  Night:         Magnesium glycinate, Casein protein, Tart cherry juice
